Adopt An Animal

Can I Call You Mom? With these plaintive words, “Babe,” the little piglet in the movie of the same name, endeared himself to us. Babe the pig was trying to find a family. And, just like little Babe, our 600+ rescued animals are hoping to find a good family…a family who cares about them and meets their needs with caring and compassion. Making a good family for the animals – after all they’ve endured before being rescued – is what our mission is all about.
